✕How Jeeto works
A little app that keeps your family close 💛 Here's the idea:
🚶
Walk together

Everyone logs their steps from any step app. Your family climbs the leaderboard as a team — not alone.

📸
Share your world

Photos, songs, birthdays, and a fresh family question every day so everyone stays close, even far apart.

🎲
Play Tambola nights

Host live Tambola and games for the whole family right inside the app. Real fun, real together-time.

💛
Care & rewards

Gentle medicine & appointment reminders, family hearts, and rewards you earn together as a household.

No money, no ads — just your family, closer. Setup takes about a minute.
